Fleshly is merging with Heltelo Labs

We are pleased to announce that we are merging with Heltelo Labs, bringing two organizations together into a common vision for the future.

This merger represents an important step forward for our company. By combining our strengths with those of Heltelo Labs, we can expand our capabilities, create new opportunities for our customers and partners, and help the job market grow overall.

Heltelo Labs is a child company of GFT Media Foundation, therefore we will maintain all employees, access to foundational resources, and so forth. Our partnership has not dissolved.

Over the coming months, expect all Fleshly resources to be moved to Heltelo. All products, research, assistance and assets will be moved to Heltelo Labs. You will be able to access all of your legacy Fleshly products in maintenance mode, even if not recommended. Heltelo will provide support for older products as long as possible.

We will continue posting updates regarding our merger. There is currently no definitive date for when the merge finishes, we will be working tirelessly to move everything over.

We hope this merger will help towards building the future together as a unified technology company for the better of the World.
