Announcing our collaboration with GFT Media Foundation

We're proud to announce our partnership with the GFT Media Foundation, a nonprofit dedicated towards funding independent artists, providing resources to misrepresented communities, and overall making the world a better place.

At Fleshly, we think beyond profit. We think about community. That's why, with every Fleshly device, we donate a sizable amount to the GFT Media Foundation. When you buy a Fleshly, you don't just get a Fleshly - you help small artists. You help families get out of poverty. Yes - you read that right.

We believe this is the pathway to achieve long-term positive impact in the world. With humanitarian crises left and right, we try to help any way we can. Our competent workforce will be volunteering their time towards helping the GFT Media Foundation achieve its goals.

We aren't doing this for public reputation, we're doing this for impact. By 2030, we aim to help over 10,000 independent artists bring their projects to life, help thousands of struggling families, and help reduce our carbon emissions by 50%. The GFT Media Foundation will also assist us in quicker production, so expect higher quality products and faster delivery times.
