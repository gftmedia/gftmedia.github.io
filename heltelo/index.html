About Us

Heltelo Labs is a for-profit subsidiary which specializes in researching and experimenting with ideas in a variety of fields, from digital to physical.

We explore ideas, prototype, and see what works. We have a simple mission: build and provide interesting, useful things.

On top of this, we conduct and fund very broad studies that can extend to a lot of fields even outside of technology.

What We Do

ML Experiments

We research the field of Machine Learning and AI to automate and perform various tasks, primarily regarding text data. We use and research various techniques of AI training and deployment.

Development and internal tooling

We develop/research internal tooling for ourselves and other companies that work with us. Our competitive engineers collaborate with other companies to help develop apps.

Contributions to the world

Our research is used in real-world scenarios to help solve critical issues, including (but not limited to) fields like medicine, data analysis and others...
